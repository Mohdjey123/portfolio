About Me

English Arabic Somali

I was born in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, but spent most of my childhood in Jeddah.

At 17 years old, I moved to Canada and finished high school here.

I initially studied Civil Engineering at Toronto Metropolitan University, but after realizing it wasn’t for me, I found my passion in computer studies at Seneca College.

I speak three languages: Arabic, Somali, and English.
